About LTS

LTS was founded in 2022. Our shop was built from a love of tacos. We hope our shop adds a unique and interesting place to our little town.

When did tacos first appeared in the United States? Jeffrey M. Pilcher, taco historian and professor at the University of Minnesota, says the earliest mention of tacos in the United States are in a newspaper from 1905.
(Source: Smithsonian Magazine)
